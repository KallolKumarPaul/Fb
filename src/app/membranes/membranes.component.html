Specialized Membranes

Untreated/Treated for Filtration Applications

Membranes are thin sheets of semi-permeable material used to separate contaminants from water when a driving force is applied. Membrane processes are increasingly used for removal of bacteria, particulates, microorganisms, and natural organic material.
